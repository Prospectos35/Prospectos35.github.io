Algerian Bourek:

  • Time: 01 h
  • Serves: 11:00_14:00
Ingredients Quantity
Sheets of dough 12
Minced meat 300 g
Onion 01
Frying oil A little
Olive oil 03 tablespoon
Grated cheese 150 g
Egg 01
Salt and pepper Pinch
Four spices 01 tablespoon
Parsley 1/2 bunch

Step 1: Brown the onion in the oil and add the meat, salt, pepper and finally the chopped parsley. Cover and cook over low heat. Once cooked, add an egg and stir. Leave to cool then add the cheese and mix. You can also add a piece of laughing cow in each puff pastry when you stuff the bourek.

Step 2: Open each sheet of dough and put the stuffing inside. Fold the two ends of the sheet and roll up the bourek. Tightening well on both sides. Brush with a little butter to close the bourek.

Step 3: Heat a little frying oil in a frying pan and fry the bourek on both sides. It's ready when they're golden brown. Enjoy with a few drops of lemon.

Source: 750 G

Couscous:

  • Time: 02 h
  • Serves: 11:00_14:00
Ingredients Quantity
Lamb meat 500g
Potatoes, peeled and quartered lengthwise 02
Carrots, peeled, cut in half 03
Large onion minced 01
Zucchini, halved and then cut lengthwise 03
Large handfuls of chickpeas, soaked the day before 02
Stalks of celery 02
Turnips peeled and cut in 4 02
Ginger Teaspoon
Turmeric Teaspoon
Cinnamon 1/2 teaspoon
Paprika 1/2 tablespoon
Salt, pepper A little
Tomatoes (canned) 05
Tomato puree dissolved in a small glass of water 01 tablespoon

Step 1: Fry the meat in oil then add the onions and cook for a few minutes. Add the blended tomatoes, the tomato concentrate as well as the spices and vegetables (carrots and celery).

Step 2: Let everything cook so that the sauce is impregnated with spices.

Step 3: Add 1.5 L of water and the chickpeas, cover and cook. When the meat has almost cooked, add the rest of the vegetables (zucchini, potato and turnip). We continue cooking.

Step 4: At the end of cooking, adjust the seasoning (add a pinch of cinnamon at the end of cooking). Place the meat on a plate. Arrange the couscous in a large deep plate, place the meat in the center, the vegetables around and drizzle with the sauce and the chickpeas.

Source: Mes Inspirations Culinaires

Rechta:

  • Time: 02 h
  • Serves: 11:00_14:00
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken pieces 04
Onion 01
Turnips 04
Zucchini 03
Salt, pepper A little
Rechta 500 g
Cinnamon 01 Teaspoon
Chickpeas soaked the day before Large handful

Step 1: Heat the oil in a pot or couscoussier, it's more practical, then add the chicken pieces and brown on all sides.

Step 2: Add grated onion and spices. Let cook for a few minutes.

Step 3: Pour 1 L of water and the chickpeas if they are soaked the day before and leave to cook.

Step 4: Pour 2 tablespoons of olive oil into the fresh rechta and check if the sauce starts to boil.

Step 5: Arrange the rechta in the top of the couscoussier and the same process as the couscous after the 1st evaporation, let it cook for 15 minutes.

Step 6: Pour the rechta into a gas3a or a large dish (a large metal cake mold).

Step 7: Remove a ladle of cooking water and sprinkle the rechta while loosening it.

Step 8: Put the rechta back in the top of the couscoussier and at the first evaporation let it cook for fifteen minutes.

Step 9: Pour a second time the rechta in a gas3a, remove the chicken or meat that is cooked and add the vegetables cut lengthwise to the sauce, let the vegetables cook.

Step 10: When the vegetables are cooked arrange the rechta in a large hollow dish and place around the vegetables and drizzle with sauce and chickpeas.

Source: Mes Inspirations Culinaires
